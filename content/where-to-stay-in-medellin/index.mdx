### Envigado: Peaceful Suburb with Local Flavor

Located south of El Poblado, **Envigado** offers a serene atmosphere with colorful streets, notable attractions like **Parque Envigado**, and authentic Colombian culture.

**Cons:**

**Best For:** Long-term stays and travelers seeking peace.
